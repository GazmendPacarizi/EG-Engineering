Mirusha River

This river is located in Malisheva.
Geometric data for this river are:

  • Length of 675.09 m;
  • Cross section form of trapezoid;
  • Base width of 8.00 m;
  • Side slopes of 1: 1.5;
  • Height of 2.00 m;
The river's channel is made by natural materials. The bed is left as it was before, while the slope sides are made of grass to prevent erosion. Sidewalks are in two sides of river and are made with layers of soil and grass.

Parking lot of Gjakova's hospital

To accommodate the needs of a hospital in the city of Gjakova, careful planning was undertaken to design a suitable parking lot. The specifications for this parking facility are as follows: The total area of the parking lot is A = 835.80 square meters, providing enough space for N = 34 parking spots. To enhance the environment, 225 square meters are dedicated to grassy areas, while an additional 163 square meters are allocated for pedestrian sidewalks. This thoughtful allocation of space ensures both functionality and aesthetics, creating a welcoming and efficient area for visitors and staff alike.
